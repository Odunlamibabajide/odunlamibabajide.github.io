About

I am a cybersecurity professional with a focus on Governance, Risk, and Compliance, certified at AltSchool Africa. Throughout my academic program, I actively translated standard controls into actionable defenses. This includes completing risk matrices for community clinics post-EHR migration and planning transnational data transfer protocols for expanding European and African fintech companies.

My background sets me apart. I have engineered real Active Directory networks, managed vulnerability profiles in Kali Linux environments, monitored threat streams using Wazuh SIEM, and established continuous cloud controls on AWS using CloudTrail and serverless remediation.

Prior to transitioning into security, I spent 13 years managing electronic records, directing health protocols, and handling operational audits. I understand that data vulnerability breaches carry direct real-world liability. I apply this rigorous compliance logic to every GRC program I support.
